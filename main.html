My Presentations and Writing

Thoughts on Advancing Yahoo Design Culture

Invited by the Yahoo Design Council, I spoke to Yahoo designers about my opinions on Airbnb's React-Sketchapp and its implications on design culture. I wanted to openly discuss expectations of a product designer and how to best work with engineering counterparts on co-producing designs.

VUI Prototyping Workshop

Given the nascency of voice assistance, I wanted to teach my design colleagues about the latest practices around prototyping. I facilitated exercises and open discussion on possible emerging patterns for a natural conversation between user and machine.

Made By Girls @ Stanford

Invited as a mentor for the day, I spoke to young girls and teens interested in STEM about how I've utilized my Computer Science degree in my design career. Made by Girls is a program that encourages girls to pursue their interests in making things (with code!) and so my talk details my journey as a maker.

YUI Conference 2013

Inspired by the building patterns behind LEGO's Taj Mahal set of 5,922 pieces, I gave a talk about prototyping to the frontend community at large. While the YUI Library community held annual conferences for engineers around the country, I advocated for a design spin when considering an architecting process.

FramerJS Workshop

Geared for an audience of designers with little to no programming experience, my tech workshop introduced designers at Yahoo to an early version of FramerJS.

Becoming a Designer

I wrote a Medium entry about how I became a UX designer, especially without formal academic training. This is geared for designer hopefuls who are contemplating a switch in their career and need to ask themselves hard and honest questions prior.

Tasting Classical Excellence: My Experience at The French Laundry

While not literally about the UX field in a technology context, this article talks about experience design based on my dining journey at The French Laundry. I often draw parallels between designing for technology and cooking, and I wanted to capture my observations and insights for foodies and design enthusiasts!

About Me

I have designed and prototyped experiences across various problem domains and devices for 9 years. A Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo Computer Science alumna, I worked in software engineering gigs at Hewlett-Packard and Apple prior to pursuing design at Yahoo. I am currently tackling the nascent world of digital assistants.

Outside of work, I explore new restaurants around the country, hone my cooking technique (Thomas Keller's Masterclass), and serve in various ministries.
