I relocated to Seattle from the San Francisco Bay Area with a new mission in tech and in poverty alleviation. Currently, I am a Senior Design Technologist in Amazon's Alexa organization where I prototype experiments and tooling and envision new North Star platform opportunities. On the side, I serve as an Advisory Board member for the Seattle Union Gospel Mission. I have been fostering a passion for homeless ministry since 2015.

I experience thrill from tinkering with things, whether it's in my day job or personal life. I'm an avid food enthusiast who enjoys experimenting with new food gadgets, procuring fun ingredients, and exploring new cooking methods to create memorable experiences at the dining table. With a mind for tech and design, I love exploring my limits as a creative person who finds joy in serving and empowering others.
