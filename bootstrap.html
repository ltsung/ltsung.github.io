I solve for problems

Experience Design for Infrastructural Engineering

I can understand the guts of technical scale. Prior to advertising, I had designed streamlined user flows for complex, information-dense interfaces responsible for large-scale infrastructural problems (e.g. datacenter hardware provisioning to reduce operational expenses and power consumption worldwide).

Cross-screen Monetization Innovation

I investigate, conceptualize, and technically assess next-generation ad formats across screens. Doing this at Yahoo's scale requires collaborating with research and design organizations across the company in order to re-imagine and evaluate meaningful advertising experiences.

Responsive Ad Platform Development

Aside from wearing a design hat, I develop with the latest frontend engineering technologies and standards. As my team is an advertising experience concept group, we are paving the way for streamlining operational inefficiencies that come with creating ad campaigns while making a dent in the responsive web space.

I share my lessons
in UX prototyping
to prompt reflection

Recently a featured speaker at the YUI Conference 2013, I shared my insights on the fundamentals of prototyping, where I ask developers to reflect upon their everyday process to craft innovative products.

I've also been invited as a regular guest speaker for my alma mater, Cal Poly San Luis Obispo, where I teach HCI and software engineering industry tips to aspiring developers and designers.

My inspiration

Outside of designing and developing for the web, I try to understand the mechanics of everyday life through art and self-tracking.

I find joy in cooking and learning more about the human experience through the culture of food. Drawing and painting have been classic past-times for me.
